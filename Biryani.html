Biryani Recipie

Ingridents

For rice

  1. 6 cup water
  2. 2 pods cardamom
  3. 4 cloves
  4. 1 inch cinnamon
  5. 2 bay leaf / tej patta
  6. 1 cup basmati rice (soaked 20 minutes)
  7. 1 tsp pepper
  8. 1 tsp salt
  9. 2 tsp oil
  10. 1 chilli (slit)

For Biryani

  1. 2 tbsp oil
  2. 1 tsp ghee / clarified butter
  3. 1 bay leaf / tej patta
  4. 1 mace / javitri
  5. 1 inch cinnamon
  6. 1 star anise
  7. 1 back cardamom
  8. 3 cloves
  9. 2 pods cardamom
  10. 1 tsp cumin / jeera
  11. ½ tsp fennel / saunf
  12. 1 onion (sliced)
  13. 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  14. 1 tomato (finely chopped)
  15. 1 potato / aloo (cubed)
  16. ½ carrot (chopped)
  17. ½ tsp salt
  18. ¼ tsp turmeric
  19. 1 tsp kashmiri red chilli powder
  20. ½ tsp cumin powder
  21. 1 tsp garam masala
  22. 1 tsp coriander powder
  23. 4 prunes / dried plums
  24. ½ tsp salt
  25. ¾ cup curd / yogurt
  26. 5 beans (chopped)
  27. 7 florets cauliflower / gobi
  28. 2 tbsp peas
  29. 5 cubes capsicum
  30. 1 cup water

For pressure cooking

  1. 4 tbsp coriander (finely chopped)
  2. 4 tbsp fried onion
  3. 2 tbsp mint / pudina (finely chopped)
  4. ¼ tsp saffron food colour
  5. ¼ cup water
  6. 1 tsp ghee / clarified butter

Let`s Begin

Biryani rice preparation:

  1. firstly, in a large vessel take 6 cup water.
  2. add spices like 2 pods cardamom, 4 cloves, 1 inch cinnamon, 2 bay leaf, ½ tsp pepper.
  3. also add 1 tsp salt, 2 tsp oil and 1 chilli.
  4. boil for 2 minutes or until flavours is in the water.
  5. add in 1 cup basmati rice and stir well. make sure to soak rice for at least 20 minutes
  6. boil for 3 minutes or until rice is half cooked. do not cook fully.
  7. drain off the rice and keep aside.

Biryani preparation:

  1. firstly, in a large kadai heat 2 tbsp oil and 1 tsp ghee.
  2. saute 1 bay leaf, 1 mace, 1 inch cinnamon, 1 star anise, 1 back cardamom, 3 cloves, 2 pods cardamom, 1 tsp cumin and ½ tsp fennel.
  3. add 1 onion followed by 1 tsp ginger garlic paste.
  4. saute until onions turn golden brown.
  5. further add 1 tomato and saute until tomatoes turn soft and mushy.
  6. now add 1 potato, ½ carrot and ½ tsp salt.
  7. cover and cook until potatoes are half cooked.
  8. further add ¼ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chilli powder, ½ tsp cumin powder, 1 tsp garam masala, 1 tsp coriander powder, 4 prunes and ½ tsp salt.
  9. saute until spices turn aromatic.
  10. additionally, add ¾ cup curd and saute until oil is released.
  11. furthermore add 5 beans, 7 florets cauliflower, 2 tbsp peas and 5 cubes capsicum.
  12. saute for a minute or until its combined well.
  13. add 1 cup water and mix well.
  14. cover and cook for 5 minutes or until vegetables are half cooked.
  15. take 1 cup of gravy and keep aside.
  16. now layer up with 2 tbsp coriander and 2 tbsp fried onion.
  17. spread half of cooked rice over the biryani gravy.
  18. top with reserved gravy uniformly.
  19. further spread remaining cooked rice and level up.
  20. top with 2 tbsp coriander, 2 tbsp mint and 2 tbsp fried onion.
  21. sprinkle ¼ tsp saffron food colour, ¼ cup water and 1 tsp ghee.
  22. cover with aluminium foil and close the lid. you can also use the dough to seal.
  23. simmer for 20 minutes or until rice is cooked fully.

  24. finally, enjoy bombay biryani with raita and slices of onion.
