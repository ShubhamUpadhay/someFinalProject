Chole Chawal Recipie

Ingridents

For pressure cooking

  1. 1 cup chana / chickpea
  2. 1 bay leaf
  3. 1 inch cinnamon
  4. 2 tea bag
  5. water ( for soaking)
  6. 3 pod cardamom
  7. 4 cloves
  8. ¼ tsp baking soda
  9. ½ tsp salt
  10. 3 cup water (for pressure cooking)

For chana Masala mix

  1. ½ tsp cumin powder
  2. 1 tsp coriander powder
  3. 1 tsp kashmiri red chilli powder
  4. ¼ tsp turmeric
  5. ¼ tsp black pepper powder
  6. 1 tsp kasuri methi
  7. ½ tsp garam masala
  8. 1 tsp aamchur
  9. pinch hing / asafoetida
  10. ¼ cup water

For curry

  1. 2 tbsp ghee / clarified butter
  2. 1 bay leaf
  3. 1 tsp cumin / jeera
  4. 1 onion (finely chopped)
  5. 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  6. 1 cup tomato pulp
  7. 2 potato / aloo (boiled and cubed)
  8. 1 tsp salt
  9. 1 cup water
  10. 2 tbsp coriander (finely chopped)

For curry

  1. 1 tsp butter
  2. 2 chilli (sliced)
  3. 2 chilli (sliced)

Let`s Begin

Preassure cooking chana

  1. firstly, soak 1 cup chana in enough water for at least 8 hours.
  2. drain off the water and transfer to the cooker.
  3. also add 2 tea bags, 1 inch cinnamon, 1 bay leaf, 3 pods cardamom, 4 cloves, ¼ tsp baking soda and ½ tsp salt.
  4. pressure cook for 5 whistles adding 3 cup water.
  5. once the pressure settles down, remove tea bags and make sure the chana has cooked completely.

chana masala mix preparation

  1. firstly, in a small bowl take ½ tsp cumin powder, 1 tsp coriander powder, 1 tsp chilli powder, ¼ tsp turmeric, ¼ tsp black pepper powder, 1 tsp kasuri methi, ½ tsp garam masala, 1 tsp aamchur and pinch hing.
  2. add ¼ cup water and mix well.

Aloo Chole Preperation

  1. firstly, in a large kadai heat 2 tbsp ghee and saute 1 bay leaf, 1 tsp cumin until it turns aromatic.
  2. also add 1 onion, 1 tsp ginger garlic paste and saute until it turns golden brown.
  3. now add prepared chole masala mix and saute for a minute.
  4. further, add 1 cup tomato pulp and saute well. to prepare tomato pulp, blend 2 ripened tomato in a blender.
  5. saute until the oil is separated from masala paste.
  6. additionally, add cooked chana and 2 boiled potato.
  7. also, add 1 tsp salt and 1 cup water.
  8. mix well adjusting the consistency as required.
  9. cover and simmer for 10 minutes or until flavours are absorbed well.
  10. prepare the tempering by heating 1 tsp butter.
  11. add 2 chilli, ¼ tsp chilli powder and saute on low flame.
  12. pour the tempering over curry and add 2 tbsp coriander.

  13. finally, enjoy aloo chole recipe with jeera rice
