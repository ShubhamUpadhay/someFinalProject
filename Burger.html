Burger Recipie

Ingridents

For Patties

  1. ½ carrot (finely chopped)
  2. 6 gobi / cauliflower (finely chopped)
  3. 4 beans (finely chopped)
  4. 2 tbsp sweet corn
  5. 2 tbsp peas / matar
  6. ¼ tsp salt
  7. 3 potato / aloo (boiled & grated)
  8. ¼ tsp cumin powder
  9. ½ tsp garam masala
  10. ¼ tsp turmeric
  11. ½ tsp kashmiri red chilli powder
  12. ¼ tsp chaat masala
  13. ½ tsp salt
  14. 2 tbsp coriander (finely chopped)
  15. ¼ cup bread crumbs

For Slurry

  1. 2 tbsp corn flour
  2. ¼ cup maida / plain flour
  3. ½ cup water
 Other Ingridents
  1. 1 cup panko breadcrumbs (for coating)
  2. oil (for frying)
  3. 3 tbsp eggless mayonnaise
  4. 2 tbsp tomato sauce
  5. 1 tsp chilli sauce
  6. 3 burger bun
  7. few slices tomato
  8. few slices cucumber
  9. pinch salt
  10. few slices jalapeno
  11. pinch pepper powder
  12. 3 slices cheese

Let`s Begin

Veggie patties Prepration

  1. firstly, steam ½ carrot, 6 gobi, 4 beans, 2 tbsp sweet corn, 2 tbsp peas and ¼ tsp salt.
  2. steam for 10 minutes on medium flame.
  3. transfer the steamed vegetables to large mixing bowl.
  4. also, add 3 boiled and mashed potato.
  5. further, add ¼ tsp cumin powder, ½ tsp garam masala, ¼ tsp turmeric, ½ tsp chilli powder,
    ¼ tsp chaat masala and ½ tsp salt.
  6. add 2 tbsp coriander and mix well combining all the spices wel
  7. furthermore add ¼ cup bread crumbs and mix well.
    breadcrumbs help to absorb moisture from vegetables.
  8. prepare a slurry by mixing 2 tbsp cornflour, ¼ cup maida and ½ cup water in a small bowl.
  9. prepare ball sized patties and dip in maida slurry.
  10. coat with panko breadcrumbs uniformly.
  11. shallow fry in hot oil keeping the flame on medium.
  12. fry on both sides, until it turns golden brown. drain off and keep aside.

Burger Prepration

  1. firstly, in a small bowl take 3 tbsp eggless mayonnaise, 2 tbsp tomato sauce and 1 tsp chilli sauce.
  2. mix well-forming burger sauce.
  3. spread a tbsp of prepared burger sauce on the bun.
  4. place a few slices of tomato, cucumber and jalapeno.
  5. sprinkle salt and pepper slightly.
  6. layer with a cheese slice.
  7. further place prepared patties and cover with another part of the bun.

  8. finally, enjoy veggie burger with chips and salad.
